FAQ

What is certificate verification?

Certificate verification is the process of confirming the authenticity and validity of a certificate issued by an organization or institution.

Why should I verify a certificate?

Verifying a certificate ensures that the certificate is genuine and has not been tampered with or forged. This is important for various purposes, such as employment, education, or professional licensing.

How do I verify a certificate?

To verify a certificate, you can enter the certificate number in the search bar on this website. The verification results will be displayed, including the certificate holder's name and the issuer's name.

What if the certificate is invalid?

If the certificate is found to be invalid, it means that the certificate may have been forged, expired, or revoked. In such cases, you should contact the issuer.
